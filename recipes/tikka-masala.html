Chicken Tikka Masala


Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 1 ½ tbsp Canola Oil
  • 1 ½ lbs Chicken Thighs
  • Black Pepper to taste
  • ½ Sweet Onion
  • 3 tbsp Tomato Paste
  • 3 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tbsp Ginger
  • 1 ½ tspn Garam Masala
  • 1 ½ tspn Chili Powder
  • 1 ½ tspn Ground Turmeric
  • 15-oz can Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Chicken Stock
  • ½ cup Heavy Cream
  • 2 tbsp Cilantro
