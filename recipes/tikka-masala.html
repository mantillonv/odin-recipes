Chicken Tikka Masala


Ingredients:

  1. 1 cup Basmati Rice
  2. 1 ½ tbsp Canola Oil
  3. 1 ½ lbs Chicken Thighs
  4. Black Pepper to taste
  5. ½ Sweet Onion
  6. 3 tbsp Tomato Paste
  7. 3 Garlic Cloves
  8. 1 tbsp Ginger
  9. 1 ½ tspn Garam Masala
  10. 1 ½ tspn Chili Powder
  11. 1 ½ tspn Ground Turmeric
  12. 15-oz can Tomato Sauce
  13. 1 cup Chicken Stock
  14. ½ cup Heavy Cream
  15. 2 tbsp Cilantro
